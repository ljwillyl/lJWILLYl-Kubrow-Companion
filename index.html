How to scan
1. Select Primary, Secondary or Tertiary below.
2. Tap 5–12 clean points in that Nexus region.
3. Repeat for all three slots.
4. If you know the correct genetics, open Confirm & Train.
The scanner uses baseline palette values plus all verified references stored in this browser. It will not automatically train from its own guesses.

Confirm this Kubrow

Only save a sample when the colour names are independently known. Incorrect confirmations reduce accuracy. Each save records the sampled LAB colour, slot, platform, settings and notes; it does not upload the screenshot anywhere.

Safe learning rules

Do save: verified imprint genetics, trusted breeder records, or a Kubrow whose colours are already confirmed by another reliable method.

Do not save: the scanner's guess unless someone has independently verified it.

Multiple platforms: keep platform and settings accurate. This lets the future shared model separate platform-specific rendering differences.
